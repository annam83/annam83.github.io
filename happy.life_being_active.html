Being active

Active living means giving your body, mind and soul the respect it deserves.

How having a dog changed my life

I definitely didin't get my dog because I wanted to stay active, but it is one of the greatest side-effects. Without having my dog I would stay in my bed, but
this way, I have to get up every morning and go outside for 30 minutes. Walking is actually one of the best ways to stay active - it doesn't require any
equipment, and literally everyone can do it. And when you have a dog it is ultimate reason to get up, put on your shoes and get outside.
