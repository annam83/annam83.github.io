Self-care

Self-care is one of the most important aspects that can help us maintain our positive mindset. We are talking about both physical and mental self-care
here

10 tips to help you stay relaxed and positive

  • meditate
  • take a hot bath
  • read a book
  • make yourself a refreshing drink
  • put on face mask
  • listen to some music
  • be creative: draw something, rearrange your room, create a website:)
  • learn a new skill, try something you always wanted to try
  • cook yourself a delicious meal
  • sing in the shower
